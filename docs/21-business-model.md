# 02 · Cómo se conectan los 9 bloques

```
KEY PARTNERS  →  KEY ACTIVITIES + KEY RESOURCES
                          ↓
                  VALUE PROPOSITION
                  ↙       ↓        ↘
       CUSTOMER REL.  CHANNELS  CUSTOMER SEGMENTS
                          ↓
                COST STRUCTURE ↔ REVENUE STREAMS
```

- Key Partners alimentan Key Activities/Resources
- Activities/Resources producen Value Proposition
- Value Prop se entrega vía Channels a Customer Segments con Customer Relationships
- Cost Structure y Revenue Streams son la consecuencia financiera

# 03 · Diferencias por tier

| Tier | Customer Segment | Customer Relationship | Channel preferido |
|------|------------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Starter $80K | Operador 1 canal · curioso | Self-service + onboarding asíncrono | Landing + Loom |
| Pro $180K | Operador 2-3 canales · serio | Onboarding asistido + check-ins | LinkedIn + referidos |
| Business $380K | Multi-canal · ticket alto | Account manager dedicado | Reuniones · pilotos |
| Enterprise | Cliente con historial complejo | Custom · contrato anual | RFP · proceso largo |
